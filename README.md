# My-Shot-Attendance-Analysis
This contains the analysis of data colletected using google sheet in order to properly plan a successful event in lagos
## Key Insight 1
- About 200+ people will be attending the event
- About 75% people are sure they will be attending the event
- 18% people are not sure if they will be attend
### Decision made: We have to make sure to find a venue that can accomodate 200+

## Key Insight 2
- 50 people are bringing just one friend
- 31 people are bringing 2-3 friends
- 4 people are looking to bring 4 friends
### Decision made: Make sure to add a 50% increase to the amount of people that are coming, i.e 300 people.
